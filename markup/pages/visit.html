VISIT US

Located at the at Kilohana Plantation in Lihue, Kaua’i, the Kōloa Rum Company Store creates the ambience of the bygone era of old plantation days, and offers our full range of our single-batch, premium Hawaiian rums, ready-to-drink cocktails, as well as tempting rum cakes, rum fudge sauce, Hawaiian Kukui Mai Tai Mix, tropical fruit jellies/jams, logo wear and a unique array of specialty gifts.

The Tasting Room, adjacent to the Company Store, offers daily scheduled tastings of our award-winning premium rums every half-hour, and has welcomed nearly 1 million people since it opened its doors in 2009. The Tasting Room accommodates up to 16 people per tasting. No reservations accepted.

The Kōloa Rum Company Store & Tasting Room celebrates our deep commitment to bring people together and expand our Kōloa Rum Ohana (family), create great memories and share the Hawaiian way of life. We are located at Kilohana Plantation, 3-2087 Kaumualii Highway, Lihue, Kaua’i. For more information, please call 808-246-8900.

All alcohol sales and tastings require a valid photo ID. Patrons must be 21 or older.
