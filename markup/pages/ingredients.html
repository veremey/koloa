Kaua’i Blossom Cocktail

Created by Ryan Sanford & Joel Downs of Oasis on the Beach — Kaua’i, Hawaii

Ingredients

1 ¼ oz.

Koloa White Rum

2 oz.

Fresh Passion Fruit Juice

1 ¼ oz.

Lavender Simple Syrup

4 - 6 oz.

Club Soda

Combine ingredients in a cocktail shaker. Pour into Collins glass.
Top with 4–6 oz. Club Soda. Garnish with an orchid.
