KOLOA RUM PREMIUM
HAWAIIAN RUM

We carefully and skillfully handcraft our premium Hawaiian rums from the finest sugarcane and the pure, abundant water sources of Kaua’i, Hawaii. Pristine rainwater from Mount Wai’ale’ale and nearby mountain peaks and rainforests is captured after filtering through volcanic strata and reaching vast underground aquifers. When combined with “tall cane” sugarcane, Kaua’i water imparts its own unique taste that perfectly complements Kōloa Rum.

Our truly unique and delicious premium rums include Kaua’i White, Gold, Dark, Spice and Coconut and Coffee Rum, as well as ready-to-drink Mai Tai, Rum Punch and Pineapple Passion products. We constantly experiment with seasonal releases in our distillery and collaborate with our neighbors to develop new and exciting offerings.
