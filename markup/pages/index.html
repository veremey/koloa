About
Kōloa rum

Since the first bottle of Kōloa Rum was distilled in 2009, we have been serving the Spirit of Aloha to the world. Made with sugarcane and rainwater from Mount Wai’ale’ale, our products preserve a history of commercial sugar production on the island of Kaua’i and harness the natural resources of Hawaii to create our delicious, award-winning rums.

learn more

WHAT MAKES Kōloa RUM UNIQUE

Made from Hawaiian sugarcane (never molasses), pristine rainwater from Mount Wai’ale’ale and twice distilled in a vintage copper pot still, our rums are remarkably smooth and rich.

WHAT MAKES KōLOA RUM UNIQUE

All distilling, blending and bottling is done in Kalaheo, Kauai. The Kōloa Rum Company embraces the rich heritage of Kauai’s sugar industry and local farmers while always looking to the future to develop new and exciting offerings.

WHAT MAKES KōLOA RUM UNIQUE

The quality of our products reflects the deep commitment of our loyal Kōloa Rum Company family to bring people together, create great memories and share the Hawaiian way of life.
